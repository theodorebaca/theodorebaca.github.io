My Collection!

Ibanez RGT2020 Prestige

This is my main guitar. It holds a special place in my heart, probably because it was my first nice guitar. Although it looks banged up and worn, it plays amazingly! I acquired it around 3 years ago and it was my first Ibanez Prestige (one of my favorite lines of guitars).

Ibanez S5521 Prestige

Here we have an Ibanez S5521 Prestige. This guitar was crafted in Japan, much like the previous guitar. However, it is much newer. This guitar was manufactured in 2015 and I purchased it new for around $1500. This thing is absolutely killer, however, I do not use it nearly as much as my RGT2020.

M2M Custom Strandberg

This is a custom shop Strandberg! It was made in Sweden and is a Made2Measure model. Basically, it is custom specs! At the time this was made, it cost around $5000, as crazy as that sounds. I actually bought this guitar used and acquired it for around $3000, which is still steep. This is a 7 string as well.

Washburn Strandberg Boden 7

This was my first Strandberg. It is a Boden 7 made by the Washburn factory in the USA. It is an amazing guitar and one of the best 7 strings I have ever picked up! The top is figured claro walnut which looks and sounds great. The neck is an endurneck.
